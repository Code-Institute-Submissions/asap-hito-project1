What, What, What in the World is DnD?

Main Required Equipment Players

What is needed to enjoy this wonderful game?

  • The Rulebook
  • An Adventure
  • Pencil and Paper
  • An Imagination
  • Roleplaying Dice

Rulebook

The Rulebook contains information about how to build your character and how to play the game. Included is lists of spells and items that will help players on their journey.

Adventure

Adventures can be created by the Dungeon Master or can be official DnD adventures.

Pencil and Paper

This game is played with pencil and paper, a character sheet is an important component of this as it records all of the Player Character's information.

Imagination

Everything takes place in the players' collective imagination.

Dice

Roleplaying dice, including the iconic twenty-sided die are key to playing this game. Each player should have their own complete set.
