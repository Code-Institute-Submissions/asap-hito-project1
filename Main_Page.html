What is DnD?

DnD or "Dungeons and Dragons" is a classic role playing game played with pen and paper. Currently in its 5th iteration, Dungeons and Dragons consists of one Dungeon Master narrating and crafting the story of the game and several Player Characters living out roles within the Dungeon Master’s world. It is easy enough for children to learn, but complex enough to take the most seasoned veterans years to master.
